Rewrite the following scene into the main character's first person view.

SCENE: The early morning sun casts a pale glow over the barren landscape of the moon. Aleksy, a grocery store clerk, is taking his regular morning walk, enjoying the tranquility of the morning. Suddenly, he hears a loud noise behind him. He turns around to see a large object hurtling towards him at high speed. He is bleeding heavily from a head wound. The impact has caused his helmet to crack, and his oxygen supply is leaking out. If he doesn't get help soon, he will die.
