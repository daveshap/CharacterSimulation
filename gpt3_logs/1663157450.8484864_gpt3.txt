Rewrite the following scene into the main character's first person view.

SCENE: Darkness falls swiftly on the moon, and Tom the bartender is getting ready to close up shop. He's been busy all day serving space tourists and weary astronauts, and is looking forward to getting home to his family. The last few customers trickle out, leaving Tom alone in the bar. He tidies up, washing glasses and wiping down the counter. Finally, he locks the door and heads home, looking up at the stars as he goes.

REWRITE AS FIRST PERSON:

==========

The darkness falls swiftly on the moon, and I the bartender am getting ready to close up shop. I've been busy all day serving space tourists and weary astronauts, and am looking forward to getting home to my family. The last few customers trickle out, leaving me alone in the bar. I tidy up, washing glasses and wiping down the counter. Finally, I lock the door and head home, looking up at the stars as I go.
