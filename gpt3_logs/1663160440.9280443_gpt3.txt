SCENE: Gabbi finished her last job of the day and started her walk home. It was a beautiful night on the moon. The air was crisp and the stars were shining bright. Gabbi was about to turn and go home when one of the aliens spotted her. They all turned to look at her and Gabbi froze. Then, one of the aliens stepped forward and held out his hand.
