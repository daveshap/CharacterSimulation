Rewrite the following scene into the main character's first person view.

SCENE: Bob is a coffee shop owner who lives on the moon. Every evening, he relaxes in his shop, sipping a cup of coffee and watching the Earth rotate slowly in the sky. He loves his quiet life on the moon, and he can't imagine living anywhere else.

REWRITE AS FIRST PERSON:

==========

I love my life on the moon. It's so quiet and peaceful here, and I love watching the Earth rotate slowly in the sky. I can't imagine living anywhere else. Every evening, I relax in my coffee shop, sipping a cup of coffee and enjoying the view.
