Rewrite the following scene into the main character's first person view.

SCENE: Tom was walking through the forest, carrying a bag of groceries. He had been visiting his family in South America and was on his way back to the airport. It was a dark night, and Tom was having a hard time seeing where he was going. Suddenly, he heard a noise in the trees. He stopped and listened, but he couldn't tell what it was. I was walking through the forest, carrying a bag of groceries. I had been visiting my family in South America and was on my way back to the airport. I started to feel a little scared, so I picked up my pace. Then I heard the noise again, and this time it sounded like it was following me. I started to run, but I was tackled from behind.
