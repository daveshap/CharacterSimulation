Rewrite the following scene into the main character's first person view.

SCENE: Wang is a delivery driver who is making a delivery to a mountaintop in Europe. He drives up to the mountaintop and sees that there is a beautiful view of the countryside. He gets out of his truck and starts to unload the boxes of goods that he is delivering. Suddenly, he hears a noise behind him. He turns around and sees a large, ferocious looking dog. The dog is growling and coming towards him. Wang drops the boxes and runs back to his truck. He gets in and drives away as fast as he can.

REWRITE AS FIRST PERSON:

==========

I was making a delivery to a mountaintop in Europe. I drove up to the mountaintop and saw that there was a beautiful view of the countryside. I got out of my truck and started to unload the boxes of goods that I was delivering. Suddenly, I heard a noise behind me. I turned around and saw a large, ferocious looking dog. The dog was growling and coming towards me. I dropped the boxes and ran back to my truck. I got in and drove away as fast as I could.
