Rewrite the following scene into the main character's first person view.

SCENE: Wang, a delivery driver, is in a bedroom, mid-afternoon. He is asleep in a chair, with a package next to him on the floor. The room is messy, with clothes and garbage strewn about. There is a strong smell of cigarettes. Suddenly, the door opens and a woman walks in. She sees Wang and gasps.

REWRITE AS FIRST PERSON:

==========

I was asleep in a chair, with a package next to me on the floor, when the door opened and a woman walked in. She saw me and gasped. The room was messy, with clothes and garbage strewn about, and there was a strong smell of cigarettes.
