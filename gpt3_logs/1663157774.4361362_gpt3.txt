Rewrite the following scene into the main character's first person view.

SCENE: Wang is driving his delivery truck through a dark forest in South America. He is tired and wants to make his delivery so he can return to his hotel and rest. He is not familiar with the area and is having trouble seeing the road. Suddenly, he hears a noise in the forest and slows down to investigate. He sees a large animal standing in the road. He does not know what it is and is afraid it will attack him. He slams on the brakes and the truck skids to a stop. The animal is a jaguar and it is about to attack Wang.

REWRITE AS FIRST PERSON:

==========

I was driving my delivery truck through a dark forest in South America. I was tired and wanted to make my delivery so I could return to my hotel and rest. I was not familiar with the area and was having trouble seeing the road. Suddenly, I heard a noise in the forest and slowed down to investigate. I saw a large animal standing in the road. I did not know what it was and was afraid it would attack me. I slammed on the brakes and the truck skidded to a stop. The animal was a jaguar and it was about to attack me.
