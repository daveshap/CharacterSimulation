Rewrite the following scene into the main character's first person view.

SCENE: Sandy was walking through the dark forest in South America, looking for her lost child. She had been searching for hours and was getting tired. Suddenly, she heard a rustling in the bushes. She froze, afraid of what might be lurking in the shadows. However, she soon realized that it was only a small animal. She breathed a sigh of relief and continued on her way.

REWRITE AS FIRST PERSON:

==========

I was walking through the dark forest in South America, looking for my lost child. I had been searching for hours and was getting tired. Suddenly, I heard a rustling in the bushes. I froze, afraid of what might be lurking in the shadows. However, I soon realized that it was only a small animal. I breathed a sigh of relief and continued on my way.
