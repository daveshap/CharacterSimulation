Rewrite the following scene into the main character's first person view.

SCENE: Bob is busy making coffee for his customers when he notices a commotion outside. He looks out the window and sees a large crowd of people gathered around a woman who appears to be in distress. He rushes out to see if he can help and finds that the woman is pregnant and about to give birth. Bob and a group of bystanders help deliver the baby and Bob offers the woman and her new baby a cup of coffee and a place to rest.

REWRITE AS FIRST PERSON:

==========

I was busy making coffee for my customers when I noticed a commotion outside. I looked out the window and saw a large crowd of people gathered around a woman who appeared to be in distress. I rushed out to see if I could help and found that the woman was pregnant and about to give birth. I and a group of bystanders helped deliver the baby and I offered the woman and her new baby a cup of coffee and a place to rest.
