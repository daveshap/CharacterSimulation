Rewrite the following scene into the main character's first person view.

SCENE: It is mid-afternoon in a dark forest in South America. Mashawn, a construction worker, is walking through the forest, when he hears a noise in the bushes. He draws his knife and approaches cautiously. Suddenly, a group of men jump out from behind the bushes, yelling and brandishing clubs. Mashawn tries to run, but they quickly catch up to him and begin to beat him. He falls to the ground, unconscious. The men take his knife and wallet, and then disappear back into the forest.

REWRITE AS FIRST PERSON:

==========

I was walking through the forest, minding my own business, when I heard a noise in the bushes. I drew my knife and approached cautiously. Suddenly, a group of men jumped out from behind the bushes, yelling and brandishing clubs. I tried to run, but they quickly caught up to me and began to beat me. I fell to the ground, unconscious.
