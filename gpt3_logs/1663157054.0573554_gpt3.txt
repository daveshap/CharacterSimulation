Rewrite the following scene into the main character's first person view.

SCENE: Jenna was in her bedroom, getting ready for bed. She turned off the lights and got into bed. She was about to drift off to sleep when she heard a noise outside her window. She got up to investigate and saw a shadow of a person outside. She was about to scream when the person put their hand over her mouth.

REWRITE AS FIRST PERSON:

==========

I was in my bedroom, getting ready for bed. I turned off the lights and got into bed. I was about to drift off to sleep when I heard a noise outside my window. I got up to investigate and saw a shadow of a person outside. I was about to scream when the person put their hand over my mouth.
