Bob is walking through the dark forest in South America, middle of the night. He is walking quickly, and seems to be looking for something. Suddenly, he hears a noise behind him. He turns around to see a large, furry creature standing there, growling at him. Bob backs away slowly, tripping over a log and falling to the ground. The creature comes closer, towering over him. Bob feels paralyzed with fear and can't move. He feels the creature's hot breath on his face as it leans in closer...
